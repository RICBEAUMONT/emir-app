Step 02

Select & Copy Your Signature

Highlight everything and copy to your clipboard

Switch to the tab where your signature just opened. Select and copy everything on the page.

  • A
    Press Ctrl + A to select everything on the page, or right-click and choose "Select All". The entire signature should become highlighted.
  • B
    Press Ctrl + C to copy — or right-click and choose "Copy".
✓ Your signature is now on your clipboard, ready to be pasted into Gmail.

Step 03

Open Gmail Settings

Access the settings cog in the top-right corner

Go to your EMIR Gmail account. In the top-right corner of the page, click the ⚙ Settings cog icon — it sits between the question mark and the 3×3 grid of dots.

▭
Screenshot · Step 03 Replace with an annotated screenshot highlighting the cog icon in Gmail's top-right toolbar

A panel will appear on the right labelled "Quick Settings". At the top of that panel, click:

— "See all settings"
▭
Screenshot · Step 03b Replace with a screenshot of the Quick Settings panel with "See all settings" highlighted
Step 04

Find the Signature Section

Scroll down and clear your existing signature

The full Settings page will now be open. Scroll down until you reach the Signature section.

▭
Screenshot · Step 04 Replace with a screenshot of Gmail Settings scrolled to the Signature section

You'll see your existing signature in the text field on the right. Before pasting the new one, this field must be emptied completely.

! Click inside the signature field, press Ctrl + A to select all, then press Delete. Confirm the field is completely blank before continuing.
▭
Screenshot · Step 04b Replace with a screenshot of the empty signature field
Step 05

Paste Your New Signature

Paste the copied signature into the empty field

Click inside the empty signature field, then paste using Ctrl + V — or right-click and choose "Paste".

✓ Your EMIR signature will appear in the field, exactly as it will look in your emails.
▭
Screenshot · Step 05 Replace with a screenshot of the signature pasted into the Gmail signature field
Step 06

Set as Default & Save

Apply your signature and save all changes

Below the signature field you will find "Signature defaults". Set your new signature for both options using the dropdowns:

  • 1
    Set "For new emails" to your new signature.
  • 2
    Set "On reply/forward" to your new signature.
  • 3
    Scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Save Changes".
▭
Screenshot · Step 06 Replace with a screenshot of Signature defaults and the Save Changes button
! Do not skip Save Changes — without saving, your signature will not be applied.
